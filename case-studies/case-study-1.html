Responsive Redesign

Context/Problem

The existing website for Brown Ballroom Dance was not mobile-friendly, causing users to struggle with navigation on smaller screens.

Research

We conducted user surveys and analyzed competitor websites to understand common issues and best practices, focusing on making the design simple and accessible.

Process

We created wireframes and prototypes, tested them with users, and iterated based on feedback, always prioritizing simplicity and user-friendliness.

Solution

The new design features a responsive layout that adapts to different screen sizes, improving navigation and user satisfaction through a clear and simple interface.

Learnings

I learned the importance of user testing in identifying usability issues early in the design process and the value of designing for accessibility.

Role

I led the design process, conducted user research, and created the final prototypes, focusing on simplicity and accessibility.
